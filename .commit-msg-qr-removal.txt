previews: remove QR badge from preview pipeline

preview is gone. New previews are clean — same 1200px JPEG, just
without the white pill and QR mark. Resolution remains the protection

on the public API for the same reason — flipping QR back on later is
a one-file change instead of a cross-call-site rewrite.
