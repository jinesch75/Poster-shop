ui: hide poster numbers, hide empty cities, shrink product preview

Three small storefront tweaks from a customer-feedback pass:

1. PosterCard — remove the small "N°XX" badge in the top-left of each
   gallery thumbnail. The number field is still kept on the Poster
   record (admin uses it for sorting + the edit form) but it's no
   longer surfaced anywhere customer-facing. The `.card .no` CSS rule
   is left in place harmlessly with no element to style — easy to
   restore later if we want the numbers back.

2. app/page.tsx — stop rendering homepage city sections that have no
   published posters yet. Previously every city in the MAIN gallery
   rendered, with empty cities falling through to a "Pictures to come"
   pane. Now the homepage only shows AVAILABLE cities that actually
   have at least one published poster, mirroring what /mondrian
   already did. New cities pop into the gallery automatically the
   moment a poster is published for them.

3. globals.css — cap the product-page main preview so it stops
   dominating the viewport. The image column went from 1.25fr to
   minmax(260px, 420px) and `.product .main-preview` got a
   max-width: 420px guard. With aspect-ratio 3/4 that's a max
   preview of roughly 420×560, which fits above the fold on a
   typical laptop without forcing the visitor to scroll. Roughly
   half the previous rendered size on a wide screen.

Three files changed: components/PosterCard.tsx, app/page.tsx,
app/globals.css.
